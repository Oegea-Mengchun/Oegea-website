Frequently Asked Questions

Here we answer the most common questions about our digital patterns and services.

What type of products do you provide? ▶

We provide digital patterns (CAD/PDF/PRJ). Customers can use them directly for production or send to factories.

Which garments can be made? ▶

Including T-shirts, shirts, skirts, jackets, etc. Custom designs are also supported.

What sizes are supported? ▶

Supports international sizes XXS/XS/S/M/L/XL/XXL, or custom sizing on request.

Can I customize special or complex designs? ▶

Yes, we provide custom designs and special size services.

How long until I receive my pattern? ▶

Standard delivery: 1–5 days. Complex designs may take longer.

What file formats are delivered? ▶

We provide PDF/CAD/PRJ files, ready to use.

How can I pay? ▶

Payments are accepted via PayPal and other international secure channels.

Is payment safe? ▶

All payments go through secure international platforms to ensure safety.

Do you offer refunds? ▶

After delivery, refunds are not available. However, major errors in files can be corrected or redone free of charge.

How to use the files? ▶

Files can be opened in design software or sent directly to factories, no extra conversion needed.

Can I use the pattern for multiple productions? ▶

Yes, the digital pattern can be used for multiple orders or factories.

How can I contact support? ▶

You can contact us via website form, email, or social media for assistance.

Do you provide technical guidance? ▶

Yes, we provide guidance for using files and adjusting sizes.
