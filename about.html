About Oegea

Oegea Pattern Studio has 8 years of professional experience in digital garment pattern making, serving independent brands and small teams worldwide. Our mission is to provide high-quality, precise, and affordable digital patterns that help designers bring their creations to life.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for fashion and technology, Oegea combines traditional garment knowledge with modern digital tools. Over the years, we have developed a streamlined process that ensures accuracy, speed, and flexibility in pattern creation.

Our Mission

We aim to empower independent designers by providing professional pattern making services that are both accessible and high-quality. Every pattern we create is tailored to the designer's needs, ensuring that their vision is accurately translated into reality.
